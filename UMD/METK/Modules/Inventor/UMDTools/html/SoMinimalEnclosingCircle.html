SoMinimalEnclosingCircle

Purpose

This module projects the vertices of the input scene onto the screen and computes the 2D bounding box to get the midpoint and the radius of these scene in screen space.

Inputs

The desired scene .

Outputs

The output is just used to get the viewport details. Nothing will be rendered.

Tips & Tricks

The position of the module in the scenegraph is arbitrary, but for a better performance it is recommended to put the module at the end.
