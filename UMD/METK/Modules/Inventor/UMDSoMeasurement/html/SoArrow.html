ModuleName

SoArrow

Purpose

Draws an arrow.

Usage

            See example network.

Details

Inputs

None.

Outputs

Inventor object: arrow.

Parameters

Geometry

·        Position
Coordinates of the arrow’s peak (head)

·        Length
Length of the arrow line

·        Display Head
On/Off head of the arrow

 

Interaction

·        Rotation Ref. Point
Coordinates of the rotation center.

 

Example

         Two arrows are rendered. The above may be rotated with center (0,0,0) around y-axis.
         Length of both arrows is 13. This doesn’t agrees with axis units (should be 10). Why?!
