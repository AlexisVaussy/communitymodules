ModuleName

           SoAngleLines

Purpose

Caclulates and renders angle between two lines in 3D. 
Angle is defined through three points StartPos, ConnectPos and EndPos.

Usage

Use with macro AngleLines2D3D to define points StartPos,ConnectPos and EndPos in a 2D display.

Outputs
