WEMCenterOfMass

Purpose

Computes the centroid of the nodes of the input WEM object.

Usage

Connect a WEM to the input field.

Details

Both the center of mass and its negated version are outputted. The last one might turn usefull if one wants to translate an object/image according to the centroid position.

Input

  • inWEM Input WEM surface

Parameters

  • centerOfMass Center of mass of WEM nodes
  • inverseCenterOfMass Negated center of mass of WEM nodes
